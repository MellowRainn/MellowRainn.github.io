FAQ

+ | How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You first need to charge your student pass with some money, then you can walk to any printer in the building, scan your student card and the document you'd like to print.

+ | How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

first need to charge your student card, then you can walk to any printer in the building, then you can send an e-mail from your laptop.

+ | How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

Go to the webshop, click on a product, add it to your shoppingcart, and to your shoppingcart and finish the purchase

+ | Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

Go to the Hz website, select current affairs, go to information about the coronavirus. Here you can see the latest updates.

+ | How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

By going to this page and reserving a spot in a room

+ | What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Go to the Hz website, go to about hz, then go to contact and location and go to the location of your school. There you will find information about parking at your school.
