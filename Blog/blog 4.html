Feedback on my first blogs

Job: Software engineer is a very general profession, Try and find a company of interest to get more of an idea what type of software engineer you would like to become and in what type of company. (what type of work do they do, culture, size etc) Keep in mind, it would be of interest to you NOW, doesn't have to be of interest in four years time.

Motivation: Trick question: Would you still have chosen ICT study when really like developing, but if there were not many job opportunities? What is more important to you? You say you are creative. How can others see you are? any specific creative hobbies you do?

SWOT Try and figure out what triggers procrastination for you. Why do you do it, knowing it does not help? what or who can you use to make it less of an impact on your study success and procrastinate less. What do you need to learn/grow into for this and how are you going to learn. (Hint: telling yourself just do is NOT going to work...)
